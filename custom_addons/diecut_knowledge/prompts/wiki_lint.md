# Task: Wiki Lint

Follow `llm_wiki_schema.md`.

Audit Wiki health and propose fixes.

Check for:

- Orphan pages.
- Missing inbound/outbound links.
- Duplicate topics.
- Missing citations.
- Contradictions.
- Stale claims.
- Title, brand, or model pollution.

Return structured findings. Do not silently overwrite human-reviewed content.
