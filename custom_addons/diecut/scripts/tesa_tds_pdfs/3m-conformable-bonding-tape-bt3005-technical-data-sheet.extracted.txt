3M™ Conformable Bonding Tape BT3005 
Technical Data Sheet 
General Description 
3M™ Conformable Bonding Tape BT3005 offers very good adhesion performance and high 
flexibility. This tape is specially designed for rough or highly irregular surfaces, providing strong 
adhesion for applications such as automotive wire harness assembly, and offering excellent 
conformability for sealing applications. 3M Conformable Bonding Tape offers a reduction in 
VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds) compared to conventional products. 
3M Conformable Bonding Tapes BT3005 offer the following key features: 
 Excellent initial adhesion and static performance on uneven substrates 
 Highly conformable tape for sealing and bonding to rough or irregular surfaces 
 Demonstrates good visibility due to its light blue color 
 Excellent adhesion performance with weather resistance, heat resistance, and chemical 
resistance 
 Can be applied to various substrates used for automotive parts without special surface treatment * 
Typical applications for 3M Conformable Bonding Tapes BT3005 include sealing or bonding support for various interior 
or exterior part attachments and fixing wire harnesses to the headliner. 
*Note: Depending on the material (e.g., EPDM rubber), 3M™ Adhesion Promoter may be required. Please check before 
use.
